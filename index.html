Sustainable Agricultural Practices

Prime Feed and Flour Mills Limited (PFFML) operates as an agro-centric enterprise, focusing on utilizing indigenous raw materials to manufacture and distribute premium product lines within Nigeria. The company's core operations encompass the production and distribution of high-quality brands in the sectors of animal feed, flour, and fish feed.

PFFM's Procedural Excellence

At PFFM, we are committed to delivering exceptional poultry feed through our unwavering dedication to Procedural Excellence. Our approach is designed to ensure that every aspect of our feed manufacturing process meets the highest standards of quality, consistency, and efficiency. This commitment drives our mission to support the health, growth, and productivity of poultry, thereby contributing to the overall success of our partners in the poultry industry.
